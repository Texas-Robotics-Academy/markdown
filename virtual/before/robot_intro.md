# Say Hello to TexBot

Introducing... TexBot!

![Robot](images/botnroll.png)

During your time in the camp, you will be getting to work on our specially designed robot, TexBot. It has a number of features that you will be using, including:

- Two wall sensors
- Two LED lights
- An "LCD" screen
- A line sensor
